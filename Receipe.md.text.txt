To make the dish follow the steps:-
1) Take the amount of kernels you need in cooker accroding to the no of people.
2) Then add olive oil into it until the kernels are covered with oil(not too much)
3)After doing the first two steps add a pich of salt for taste and little turmeric powder for colour!.
4)Then put the cooker on a low flame until the popping kernel's stop.
5) After the kernel's stops to pop transfer the pop corn into a bowl and your delicious pop corn is ready!!!!
